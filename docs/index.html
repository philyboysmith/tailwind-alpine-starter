Why

Everything we value and rely on in life depends on nature: a stable climate, food systems, livelihoods, education, health and wellbeing. With over 50% lf of the global GDP $44 trillion threatened by nature loss, protecting and regenerating nature is the single most effective thing we can do to address climate change. A ‘nature positive economy’ means reversing biodiversity loss by 2030 and has the potential to unlock $10 trillion of business opportunities across food, infrastructure and energy. This is why we build teams who...

  • Mobilise nature-based investments
  • Integrate nature into business models and strategy
  • Prioritise nature in policymaking and regulation
02

Our Story

Amid the rapidly intensifying challenges of climate change and environmental degradation, our founder Hannah Wylie, recognised the need for a talent acquisition firm to drive systemic change.

By building specialised nature-based talent pools across finance, business, tech, policy, and law, we aim to be that catalyst.

With a background in financial risk and regenerative agriculture, our founder understood that investors and leaders with expertise in forestry, agriculture, marine science, conservation and STEM are crucial for mobilising capital and unlocking NbS (Nature-based Solutions) investment opportunities across natural capital, infrastructure, and energy.

In response, ST was established in 2015 to meet this need. Our team, with combined experience in finance, oceans, agriculture, forestry, extractives, and emerging markets, excel in delivering on challenging global mandates. We build teams that achieve risk, return and impact.
